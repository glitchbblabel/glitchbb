the mission

find the artists making music that sounds like a memory you can't place — and give it a home. glitchbb exists to press play on the stuff the algorithm skips: dream loops, broken breaks, songs built at 3am for an audience of one. we put them somewhere they can be found.

why this exists

because the internet used to feel like somewhere. labels got optimized into spreadsheets and music got optimized into content. glitchbb is the opposite bet: small roster, strange sounds, real care.

if a song makes you feel like an old file you forgot you saved — it belongs here.

about the label

glitchbb is artist-first and internet-native. releases live wherever you listen; the weird stuff lives here. the roster grows slowly, on purpose — every channel has to earn its number.

send a demo

demos are open. if your music sounds like it came from a machine that loved you back, tune in to channel 99.
